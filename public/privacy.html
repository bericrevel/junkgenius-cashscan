Privacy Policy

JunkGenius CashScan · Effective July 2026

The short version: you point your camera at junk, we send that one photo to an AI to figure out what it's worth, and then it's gone. Your sales numbers stay on your phone. We don't run ads, we don't sell data, and we can't see your card number.

That's genuinely the whole story. The details below just say it slower.

What we collect, and why

DataWhenWhy & where it goes
A photo Only when you tap SCAN IT or pick one from your gallery Sent over an encrypted connection to our server, which passes it to Anthropic's AI to identify the item and estimate its value. We don't store it. It exists to answer your question, then it's gone from our systems.
An anonymous device ID Created the first time you open the app A random ID (just letters and numbers — nothing about you or your phone) used to check whether this device has a Pro subscription. It's stored with your subscription record at Stripe.
Your email Only if you use "Restore my Pro" Used once to find your subscription at Stripe and reconnect it to your new phone. We don't keep a copy — Stripe already has it from your original checkout.
Subscription status If you buy Pro We check with Stripe whether your subscription is active, so the app knows to stay unlocked. We never see or store your card details — payment happens on Stripe's own secure page in your browser.

What never leaves your phone: your sales ledger — every item you mark sold, every dollar you track — is stored only on your device. We can't see it. It isn't synced, uploaded, or backed up anywhere by us. Deleting the app deletes it.

What we don't do

  • No ads and no advertising trackers.
  • No selling or renting data. Not to anyone, not ever.
  • No analytics SDKs tracking what you do in the app.
  • No location tracking. The app never asks where you are.
  • No contacts, no accounts, no passwords. There's nothing to sign up for.
  • No tracking links. The QR codes on our flyers count total scans anonymously — a number goes up. Nothing about you or your phone is recorded.

About the AI step

Item identification, repair guides, and listing text are generated by Claude, an AI service by Anthropic. Your photo and the item details are processed under Anthropic's commercial API terms, which do not use this data to train their models. Anthropic may retain API data briefly for abuse monitoring before deletion — see Anthropic's privacy policy.

One honest caution that's also in the app: AI value estimates and repair guidance can be wrong. Use your judgment, especially around anything electrical, fuel-powered, or sharp.

Payments

CashScan Pro payments are handled entirely by Stripe, on Stripe's own checkout page in your browser. Your card number never touches this app or our servers. You can cancel anytime from the "Manage / cancel subscription" button in the app.

Deleting your data

  • On your phone: uninstalling the app permanently deletes your ledger, settings, and device ID.
  • At Stripe: if you subscribed and want your billing records deleted, email us (below) from your checkout email and we'll handle it, subject to records we're legally required to keep (like tax records of payments).
  • Photos: nothing to delete — we don't store them.

Security

Everything the app sends travels over encrypted connections (HTTPS). Our AI and billing keys live on our servers, never inside the app.

Children

CashScan is not directed at children under 13, and we don't knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes

If this policy changes in any way that matters, we'll update this page and the effective date above. We won't quietly get worse.
