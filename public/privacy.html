Privacy Policy

JunkGenius · Effective July 2026

The short version: you point your camera at junk, we send that one photo to an AI to identify and price it, and then it's gone. Your inventory and every dollar you log live on your phone. We don't run ads, we don't sell data, and we can't see your card number.

That's the whole story. The details below just say it slower.

What leaves your phone, and why

DataWhenWhere it goes
A photo Only when you tap SCAN IT or pick from gallery Encrypted to our server, passed to Google's Gemini AI to identify every item in the frame and estimate its value. We don't store it — it answers your question and it's gone from our systems.
An item's details Only when you tap "Show me how" for repair/scrap/part-out guidance Sent to our server and Anthropic's Claude AI to write that guidance. Nothing is stored beyond answering that one request.
An anonymous device ID Created on first launch A random ID (nothing about you or your phone) used to check whether this device has a Pro subscription. Stored with your subscription record at Stripe.
Your email Only if you use "Restore my Pro" Used once to find your subscription at Stripe and reconnect it. We keep no copy — Stripe already has it from your own checkout.

What never leaves your phone: your inventory, every dollar you log, and how many times you've scanned. We can't see any of it. Deleting the app deletes it.

JunkGenius is under active development. Planned additions — a chat assistant, a yard/spot finder that would use your location, live market comps, and a listing generator — will each get their own row added here, and their own honest empty-state until they're real, before they ship. Nothing in that list is collecting anything from you today.

What we don't do

  • No ads and no advertising trackers.
  • No selling or renting data. Not to anyone, not ever.
  • No analytics SDKs tracking what you do in the app.
  • No background location. The app doesn't currently request location at all.
  • No accounts, no passwords. There's nothing to sign up for.

About the AI

Photo identification and valuation are handled by Google's Gemini API. Under Gemini's commercial API terms, prompts and images sent through the paid API are not used to train Google's models, and are retained only briefly (by default, up to 55 days) to detect abuse before deletion — see Gemini's API terms and Google's privacy policy.

Repair/scrap/part-out guidance is written by Anthropic's Claude, under commercial API terms that similarly don't use this data to train models, with brief retention for abuse monitoring before deletion — see Anthropic's privacy policy.

Values are estimates, not appraisals, and the app says so wherever they appear. One honest caution that's also in the app: AI estimates and guidance can be wrong. Use your judgment, especially around anything electrical, fuel-powered, or sharp.

Payments

JunkGenius Pro payments are handled entirely by Stripe, on Stripe's own checkout page in your browser. Your card number never touches this app or our servers. Cancel anytime from "Manage / cancel subscription" in the app.

Deleting your data

  • On your phone: uninstalling permanently deletes your inventory, your logged cash, and your device ID.
  • At Stripe: if you subscribed and want billing records deleted, email us from your checkout email — subject to records we must legally keep (like tax records of payments).
  • Photos: nothing to delete — we don't store them.

Security

Everything the app sends travels over encrypted connections (HTTPS). Our AI and billing keys live on our servers, never inside the app.

Children

JunkGenius is not directed at children under 13, and we don't knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes

If this policy changes in any way that matters, we'll update this page and the date above. We won't quietly get worse.
